Let me share the foods I love with you!

I love food. I have lots of favourite foods but I will specifically pick some of my favs!! :)

Specifically what version of the dish I like most?

Potato

I love potato, in various ways the dish is prepared. I like it mashed, fried, and it as potato chips.

Pasta

I LOVEE pasta!!! I do not like it overly-creamy, just in between. I love bolognese pasta, carbonara pasta, etc.

Hotpot

The soups make the hotpot savoury and delicious. I like mala, its spicy and good at the same time. I also like the tomato base soup, so good!!

Cookies

Chewy and thick cookies!! I love smores chocolate chip cookies and the classic choco chip cookies. It is just that good.
